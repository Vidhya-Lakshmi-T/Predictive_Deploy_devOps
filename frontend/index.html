Trigger User Confusion Signals

Use these controls to simulate user frustration. The tracker batches signals every 5 seconds and sends them to the backend.

Rage Clicks
Click 4 or more times rapidly in the same spot to fire a `rage_click` signal.
Repeated Actions
Click the same action 3 or more times to fire `repeated_action`.
Scroll Oscillation
Rapid up/down reversal creates a `scroll_oscillation` signal.
Idle Hesitation
Stop interacting for 5 seconds to fire `idle`.
Force Auto Rollback
Sends rage click + scroll + repeated action + idle in one batch so the prediction jumps to HIGH and rollback executes immediately.
